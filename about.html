About Us

About Us

Dyen is a network focused on giving youths FREE access to the right skills, support, and confidence to build a successful future.

Our Story

Our Story

2020

How It Started

In 2020, we held a free training to show young people simple ways to start making money online. Surprisingly, it went well. And that was when we realised there was a huge skill gap among the youths — they needed to be taught the right skills to succeed online. So, after much planning, we decided to start up a network that gives youths full access to these skills without paying hefty tuition fees: The Dominion Youth Empowerment Network.

Today

Where We Are Now

We've gone ahead to train more than 4,200+ youths, placed 845+ interns, and awarded scholarships worth more than ₦1bn. Best part? Most of our students have been able to build a solid career out of the skills we taught them. Some are award-winning chefs who now have their own restaurant, while some have even travelled abroad and are currently crushing it in the foreign market.
