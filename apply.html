FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the training really free?
+
Yes, completely free. DYEN covers all tuition costs through scholarships. You will never be asked to pay for your training.
Who is eligible to apply?
+
Any Nigerian youth between 16 and 35 years old can apply. No prior experience is required — just passion and commitment to show up.
Where are classes held?
+
Classes are currently held physically in Warri, Delta State. We believe strongly in the power of face-to-face learning.
How long does each program last?
+
Programs range from 2 to 4 months depending on the course. You will receive a detailed schedule upon admission.
Will I get a certificate at the end?
+
Yes! Students who complete the program and meet attendance requirements receive an official DYEN certificate of completion.
What happens after the program?
+
DYEN supports graduates through internship placements and networking events to help you connect with employers, clients, and fellow graduates.
